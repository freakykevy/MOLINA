Dinakdakan Recipe

A traditional Ilocano dish made of grilled pork parts, mixed with onions, chili, and creamy dressing.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg pork face (maskara), ears, and pork belly
  • 1 red onion (chopped)
  • 2–3 green chilies (siling haba), sliced
  • 2 pcs red chili (siling labuyo), chopped (optional for spice)
  • 3 tbsp vinegar or calamansi juice
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise (or boiled pig’s brain, traditional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps to Cook

  1. Grill pork face, ears, and belly until cooked and slightly charred.
  2. Let them cool, then slice into small bite-sized pieces.
  3. In a bowl, combine grilled pork, onions, and chilies.
  4. Add vinegar (or calamansi juice), salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  5. Stir in mayonnaise (or pig’s brain for authentic version) until creamy.
  6. Serve as pulutan (beer match) or with steamed rice.

Cooking Notes

Grilling
Charcoal grilling gives the best smoky flavor.
Dressing
Traditionally, pig’s brain is used for creaminess, but mayonnaise is a modern substitute.
Serving
Best enjoyed fresh, as a pulutan with drinks or as a viand with rice.
